Description

Lasagna is an Italian classic for the ages. Originating from Naples, Italy in the Middle Ages. Most people you know will have childhood memories of a homecooked lasagna. Personally, it brings me memories of my Mum making us Lasagna on our birthday dinner nights.

The rich tomato sauce breaking up the crisp pasta layers, it's a dish that anybody should have at least once in their life. Without further ado, how to make a lasagna.

Ingredients:

  • Meat: Italian sausage and lean ground beef. (Use mushrooms for a vegetarian alternate)
  • Aromatics: An onion and two cloves of garlic to cook with the meat.
  • Tomatoes: A can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce and two cans of tomato paste. For the sauce.
  • Sweetness: At least two tablespoons of white sugar to balance the tomatoes.
  • Seasonings: Fresh parsley, dried basil, salt, italian herbs, fennel seeds and black pepper. For a bit of flavour
  • Pasta: Lasagna sheets, fresh or store bought.
  • Cheese: Parmesan, mozzarella and ricotta cheese.
  • Egg: A single egg.
